Civitate Futurium

Designing the Future

Government Buildings

The Central Tower is the main political office of Civitate Futurium. The tower is 200 hundred stories tall, and contains multiple assembly rooms in the base of the tower.

Citizen Residence

Residencial areas are scattered around the Civitate Futurium, usually built near private businesses and public areas. Most residences are in the form of apartments, as there is limited land in the city. Despite the slightly decreased living space in Civitate Futurium, the top floors of apartment towers offer breathtaking views of the industrial beuaty down below, and also vies of the warm, promising glow of the eastern sunrise.

Private Businesses

Many of Civitate Futurium's tall skyscrapers are private businesses. Along with government- funded organizations, many private companies specialise in mechanical engineering and software development. Companies such as Mikrosoft, Intell, Toyoeta, and SpaceEcks all have their headquarters located in Civitate Futurium. The city's income is also 30% higher than any other American Union State City's income.

Public Areas

The urbanized environment of Civitate Futurium is not without natural life. There are a bird feeders every half- mile or so, and parks are filled with lush, green grass and beautiful trees. Local community centers work hard to provide a wide variety of activities for the young, middle- aged, and old.

Resource Supplies

Civitate Futurium holds control of the world's largest fresh water supply, a huge underground cave 80 miles squared, filled with water. There are also a number of nuclear generators in the city, along with lab- grown meat, grains, vegetables, and other scientifically proven healthy GMO's.

Transportation Network

The complex network of roads and transport systems is very well developed. Hundred of thousands of cars drive these roads every day, while Transport Capsules carry passengers from one point of the city to another.
